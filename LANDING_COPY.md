## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes. No login, no credit card, no catch. Run the audit and get your results instantly. We ask for your email after we show you the savings — never before.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
Every number is manually verified against official vendor pricing pages during the submission week and cited in PRICING_DATA.md. Enterprise pricing (Cursor Enterprise, Claude Enterprise, ChatGPT Enterprise) is estimated from public sources — we flag these clearly in the audit output.

**Q: What does Credex get out of this?**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits to startups. This tool helps you find where you're overspending — for high-savings audits, we surface Credex as a way to capture even more savings through credits sourced from companies that overforecast. It's a lead generation tool. We're transparent about that.

**Q: Is my data private?**
Your audit is saved with a unique URL. The public version of that URL shows your tools and savings numbers but never your email, company name, or role — those are stored separately and never exposed on the shared link.

**Q: What tools do you support?**
ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, Anthropic API direct, and OpenAI API direct. We cover the tools that account for the majority of AI spend at early-stage startups.
